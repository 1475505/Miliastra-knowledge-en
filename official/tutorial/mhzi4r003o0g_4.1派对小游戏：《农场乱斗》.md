# 4.1 Party Game: "Farm Frenzy"

## Tutorial Description:

In this tutorial series, we will use multiplayer casual gameplay as an example for practical exercises, connecting and explaining the comprehensive usage of various editor modules, to build a basic playable template version. You can later iterate and optimize it based on your personal design preferences and add other gameplay features.

_**Throughout the tutorial series, we will explain the design of each module step by step according to the design sequence. Since the modules are interconnected,_**_it is recommended to follow the tutorial sequence for first-time learners_**_to avoid issues with understanding tutorial content and maintaining continuity due to skipping around._

## Related Information:

**Gameplay Introduction:**In a square farm, players cultivate, plant, and harvest crops within a time limit. Players collect ripe fruits and deliver them to the shop to earn points. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.

**Game Perspective:**Top-down view

**Number of Players:**2-4 players, each in their own team

**Game Duration:**5min/round

**Special Game Mechanics:**

> **[Resource Competition]**Each player's area has a rake and watering can. Players can only hold one tool at a time and can "steal tools" from opponents' areas. Seeds spawn periodically based on the number of players. Players can only carry one seed pack at a time but can strategically store extra seeds in their area to increase the cost for opponents.
> 
> 
> **[Crop Stealing]**During cultivation, players can only plant in their own area but can "steal crops" from opponents' areas.

The above is an overview of the gameplay covered in this tutorial series. After completing the series, you can expand the functionality with features such as: weather system, more diverse items, differentiated map design, random events, etc., to enhance gameplay and entertainment.
