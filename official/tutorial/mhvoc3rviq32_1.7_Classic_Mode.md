### Feature Overview

A new **Character Settings** section has been added to the basic system presets, allowing you to configure the **Number of Principal Cast Members** and **Character Restrictions**.

![Image 7](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/c5e82cd1-5749-4760-8390-9638e100d7c8.png)

#### 1. Number of Principal Cast Members

This setting determines the maximum number of characters a player can assign to their party for the current stage. By default, a single player can deploy up to 4 characters, two players can each deploy up to 2 characters, and when there are more than two players, each player is limited to 1 character. You may adjust the first two values to define the maximum number of characters players are allowed to deploy.

#### 2. Character Restrictions

Changes character availability from unrestricted to restricted.

![Image 8](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/6ed76843-55a4-474d-9c6e-ef0e854a4fcd.png)

Click "+" to add characters that are permitted to enter the stage.

![Image 9](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/9504b9a7-d1d4-4116-930c-eabaee7cf896.png)

In this interface, you can specify which characters are permitted to enter the stage. The Traveler is used as an example.

![Image 10](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/7bcff2f7-721b-4e2c-95f7-49beb47868db.png)

After exiting the interface, the Selectable Characters list will include the Traveler. This indicates that the stage is restricted to Aether only. The effect of this restriction is demonstrated in the following playtest section.

![Image 11](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/06776878-4418-463d-8cb4-43d6c9037548.png)

You can also use the filters in the top-left corner to narrow characters by element or weapon type. After filtering, use Select All to add all matching results at once, or Cancel All to clear the current filtered selection.

![Image 12](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/69d286ec-cb70-4e72-a860-fe21d59600b0.gif)

## Feature Example: Modifying Character Energy Based on Interaction Option

**Feature:**After the player selects an interaction option, Energy is updated: option 1 sets Energy to 100, option 2 sets Energy to 0, option 3 adds 10 Energy, and option 4 subtracts 10 Energy.

**Effect Demonstration:**

In this demo, the Anemo-aligned Traveler's Elemental Burst requires 60 Energy. Changing the character's Energy through node logic causes the Elemental Burst icon to update accordingly.

_**The text shown in this demonstration requires unlocking the Custom Text Display feature at a specific Craftsperson Level. This tutorial focuses on showcasing the effect only._

![Image 19](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/5753a86c-a105-4397-b810-9ed5834ea33e.gif)

1. Create a prefab as an interactive object.

![Image 21](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhndimifft7g/b96cbc42-05b1-4cb9-bdc6-edcf48f1da74.png)
