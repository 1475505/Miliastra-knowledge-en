![Image 4](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/c015a05c-0a94-4b09-b539-4dd62d38dbeb.png)

## II. How to Download and Use Assets

### 1. Search and Filter Assets

**1. How to Search for Assets**

Enter keywords or author names in the search bar above to find the stage you want (using the Wonderland asset "Combat Skill 1" as an example).

![Image 5](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/c017f46f-7869-4d95-8343-f77bd7c8e4a6.png)

**2. Filter and Sort Assets**

You can filter the stages you need in the following ways

①Filter asset type

②Filter by Release Time

③Sort by Popularity

![Image 6](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/8c617d39-ae1d-418b-aed1-96b399ce0562.gif)

### **2. Download Assets**

**1. Download from Preview Screen**

Once you find the asset type you want, click the download button in the upper-left corner to download it.

![Image 7](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/b7cc5827-25e6-40d4-b80f-0ca4e4792b78.gif)

**2. Download from Details Page**

Click the desired asset type. Click "download asset" button to download the corresponding file.

![Image 8](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/8e6112af-aae9-4224-bfaf-55f33049150a.gif)

### **3. Import Assets**

**1. Enter [In-Game Editor]**

In the editor, click ![Image 9](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/a3f8e11e-20a4-4776-8ae7-1ddefb812098.png), and select the [Manage Asset Import/Export] button

![Image 10](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/4cbd2b9d-8015-44ee-9b0b-85fd0fd7f66c.png)

**2. Enter Manage Asset Import/Export**

Click [Load External Assets] button, then select [Open Directory].

![Image 11](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/58037b0f-1740-4704-b1ee-fc43b1609d05.png)

**3. Move Files to Corresponding Directory**

Copy and paste the assets you want to import into this directory.

![Image 12](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/44a52497-af5b-4fab-869d-fa3d70e42850.png)

**4. Load Assets in Game**

Return to the game and press the Load External Assets button

![Image 13](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/632146e4-b1e5-4d5d-b852-34067680f3d0.png)

**5. Save the assets you need**

After loading is complete, you can see the corresponding assets. Click on the assets you need to transfer to the stage, then click "Transfer Selected Assets"

![Image 14](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/cdf67443-bdc2-4ca0-81bc-27b6c1951db4.png)

**6. Select the Tab to Transfer**

Select the tab to transfer. You can place it in the default tab or choose to create a new tab for importing assets.

![Image 15](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/fff799d6-0bd4-4495-9ad9-fdd64f75e702.png)

**7. View Imported Assets in the In-Game Editor**

You can view your imported assets in the corresponding location

![Image 16](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/891b1e30-b264-4f6f-8a80-f93c2feba06b.png)

### **4. Import Stage/Save**

**1. Enter Save Settings**

Click the top-left corner of the in-game editor![Image 17](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/bbcf8a72-10e2-4a81-ad71-35b1a66005f1.png)(or press ESC) to bring up the left panel and ESC menu bar, then click![Image 18](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/86af3438-8a50-41b1-b771-ff17b868538a.png) to open

![Image 19](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/5cfcd262-1096-4180-9097-5b6adecf6c96.png)

**2. Export Save File**

Click "Save As" to export the stage, name the save file, and save it to the corresponding folder.

![Image 20](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/fcee1b49-24e2-4894-b31f-12ee7d6efe84.png)

### **3.**Asset Status Management

**1. Go to Profile**

Click the profile picture in the upper-right corner to access your profile.

![Image 31](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/e431869c-fdd7-4d09-980c-04e7a97c4e88.png)

**2. Check Asset Information**

You can check the download, favorite, and like counts for all assets. You can also check the download, favorite, and like counts for each individual asset in [My Assets].

![Image 32](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/20d8264b-b9ea-45e4-a0d3-9a0700d7503f.png)

**3. Asset Status Adjustment**

You can manage the status of each asset here, including delisting assets, editing asset information, and other related actions. You can also re-upload delisted assets from the Recycle Bin.

![Image 33](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/6f2ff93a-026d-48c8-9495-f599cb54c51a.png)

**4. View Review Status and Publication Results**

Check system notifications such as asset review results in the Message Center to quickly see whether your submission has been approved. Assets that fail the review can be modified in the Recycle Bin and resubmitted for review.

![Image 34](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/course/sea/en-us/mhbciq9xs5qq/19a5327b-84f7-49a3-9415-8b6af673d424.png)
