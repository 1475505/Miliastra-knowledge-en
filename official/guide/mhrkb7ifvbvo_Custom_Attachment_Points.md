# Custom Attachment Points

# I. Functions of Custom Attachment Point Component

_Attachment points_ refer to specific positions on the skeleton or structure of an _entity_, used to attach other _units_ or _special effects_.

The custom attachment point component allows creators (Craftspeople) to freely choose a position as the unit's attachment point

After adding such attachment points, their offset from the center origin is fixed and will not change relative displacement with the entity's skeletal movement

Multiple custom attachment points can be added to the custom attachment point components

# II. Editing Custom Attachment Point Components

## **1. Add** Unit Attachment Point

![Image 2](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/knowledge/sea/en-us/mhrkb7ifvbvo/4fb0e884-09a5-4d75-99f3-c300538798f2.png)

(1) In the entity/prefab editing interface, open the Editing Components tab

(2) Click "Add Common Components" below, select and click "Custom Attachment Point" to add it

(3) Click "Advanced Editing" to expand the editing tab

## **2.** Add Custom Attachment Points

On the details tab of the custom attachment point component, you can press [Add Custom Attachment Point] to add a new attachment point

![Image 3](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/knowledge/sea/en-us/mhrkb7ifvbvo/aad6e6bb-5d71-4618-ad7f-8d91705a0802.png)

## **3.** Configure Custom Attachment Points

![Image 4](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/knowledge/sea/en-us/mhrkb7ifvbvo/9d9a3893-0696-4859-8191-403012a91140.png)

_Attachment Point Name_: The name for the attachment point, which can be referenced by other systems

_Offset_: The position offset of the attachment point relative to the prefab or entity center origin

_Rotation_: The orientation offset of the attachment point relative to the prefab or entity center origin
