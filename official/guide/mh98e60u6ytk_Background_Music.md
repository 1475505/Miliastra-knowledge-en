# Background Music

# I. Functions of Background Music

Through Background Music Management, Creators (Craftspeople) can customize background music for their gameplay. During subsequent gameplay, it can also be edited via the Node Graph

In Co-Op Mode, different players can have different background music

## 2. Detailed Introduction

![Image 4](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/knowledge/sea/en-us/mh98e60u6ytk/54c7b5c3-bfb0-4b7e-98aa-14fd39423e51.png)

_Background Music Assets_: Allows selecting a specific background music asset from the Music Library

![Image 5](https://act-webstatic.hoyoverse.com/ugc-tutorial/knowledge/sea/en-us/mh98e60u6ytk/11181e33-e8fd-4a40-b05f-ef051a694fac.png)

_Effective Time Range_: Specifies the portion of the full music asset to be used as the actual background music playback

_Volume_: Configures the background music volume

_Playback Speed_: Default is 1. Adjusts the playback speed of the background music. Extremely fast or slow values may affect the listening experience

_Fade In/Out_: Determines whether fade-in and fade-out effects are applied at the start and end of playback

_Loop Playback_: Determines whether the background music restarts automatically after finishing

_Interval Time (s)_: If loop playback is enabled, waits for the specified interval after playback ends before playing again
