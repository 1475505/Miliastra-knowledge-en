**IV. Beyond Mode and Classic Mode**

_Beyond Mode_: Only supports players using Manekins to enter levels for gameplay. All data can be defined by the creator (Craftsperson) within the level, with a maximum of 8 players.

_Classic Mode_: Allows players to use any characters they own in Teyvat to enter levels for gameplay (players can bring multiple characters, retaining their attributes, skills, talents, weapons, artifacts, and constellations; the creator (Craftsperson) cannot modify this part of the data), with a maximum of 4 players.

The main differences between the two modes are:

In Classic Mode, there are no features such as class settings, character skills, custom aggro systems, load optimization, shield mechanisms, Wonderland Gift Boxes, character level adjustments, scan tags, main camera management, skill resource management, in-level save management, and character disruptor devices. Additionally, new factions cannot be created, and existing factions cannot be dynamically modified.

In the comprehensive guide, unless otherwise specified, content not mentioned above is available in both Beyond Mode and Classic Mode.

For other features that require special mention due to unique circumstances, additional explanations will be provided in the guide.
