# II. Feature Details

## 1. In the Editing Interface

Click the Undo and Restore buttons to revert or reapply recent operations

The undo history is limited to 50 steps. Actions beyond this limit will not be recorded

## 2. Inside the Miliastra Sandbox

Click the Undo and Restore buttons to revert or reapply recent operations

The undo history is limited to 20 steps. Actions beyond this limit will not be recorded

Players can click any step in the history list to undo/restore that step
